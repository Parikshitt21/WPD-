Famous Landmarks in Pune

Pune Metro 🚇

The Pune Metro is a modern transit system connecting major parts of the city, ensuring faster and smoother travel for citizens and tourists alike.

Mumbai-Pune Expressway 🛣️

This expressway is India's first six-lane concrete highway, linking Pune and Mumbai efficiently, making road trips easier and enjoyable.

Dagdusheth Halwai Ganpati Temple 🐘

A prominent Ganesh temple known for its grandeur and spiritual significance, especially vibrant during Ganesh Chaturthi celebrations.

Shaniwar Wada 🏰

Shaniwar Wada is a historical fortification with impressive architecture, famous for its role in the Maratha Empire and its captivating sound and light show.

Sinhagad Fort 🏯

A popular trekking destination, Sinhagad Fort offers breathtaking views, local food stalls, and rich historical significance as a Maratha stronghold.

Saras Baug 🌳

Saras Baug is a beautiful garden and temple complex, ideal for relaxing strolls, family picnics, and exploring Pune's serene side.

Red Palace 🟥

The Red Palace, also known as Lal Mahal, is an iconic structure tied closely to the life of Shivaji Maharaj and a symbol of Pune's proud heritage.
